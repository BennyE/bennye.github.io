About OV Caddy

OV Caddy helps with onboarding your OmniAccess Stellar Wireless Access Points (AP) to OmniVista Cirrus (4.x) tenants either directly or Multi-Tenancy Service (MTS) managed. The application allows to scan the Stellar Wireless APs QR code directly from the shipping package or from the APs label on the back. You can optionally include the GPS location (enabled in QR Code scan view via button) when installing the Stellar Wireless AP to benefit of a precise installation location on customer premises.
