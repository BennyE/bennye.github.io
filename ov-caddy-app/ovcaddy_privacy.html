Collection of personal data

OV Caddy doesn't collect personal information individually nor allows to register accounts with OmniVista Cirrus. This means that accounts to access OmniVista Cirrus need to be created in the corresponding solution beforehand. Please refer to detailed information about Alcatel-Lucent OmniVista Cirrus on their official website.

Process of onboarding an OmniAccess Stellar Wireless Access Point

If you choose to enable "GPS Position" while onboarding/scanning an AP, the GPS position of the device will be recorded in the onboarding request in OmniVista Cirrus.
